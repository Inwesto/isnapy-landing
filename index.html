Not another passive feed.

The idea is simple: a video is not just uploaded and forgotten. It has a state, power, history, and future. Audience actions change what happens next.

🎥

Upload short videos

Creators upload clips that become active objects inside the iSnapy world: visible, measurable, and ready for interaction.

🥊

Enter battles

Videos can challenge other videos, win exposure, gain status, or get wounded after risky fights.

✨

Become alive

Stickers, shields, cracked states, boosts, and revival mechanics make each video feel like a living object.

Core mechanics

Built first as a clean MVP, with deeper fate, ranking, and lifecycle logic added step by step.

  1. 1Video enters the feedEvery clip starts with base power and simple interaction counters.
  2. 2Audience changes its fateLikes, comments, shares, views, and watch signals influence growth and future scoring.
  3. 3Battle mode creates tensionVideos can challenge similar-power opponents or take risky fights for bigger upside.
  4. 4Lifecycle controls costExposure caps, resting states, and fate logic can keep the platform fair and efficient.
